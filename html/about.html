From an early age, my fascination with computers was ignited when my father introduced me to CDs and DVDs on a PC that came with educational books. By the time I reached third grade, I had my computer, and my journey of exploration truly began. Through countless trials and experiments, I developed a deep passion for technology—sometimes learning the hard way. In 2018, driven by curiosity about Ubuntu after seeing it on my school’s smart boards, I attempted to install it on my PC, only to accidentally wipe the entire hard drive. Undeterred, I continued experimenting, even disassembling my PC to troubleshoot hardware issues—an experience that taught me invaluable problem-solving skills.
Over the years, I have cultivated programming, game development, and software engineering expertise. I enjoy solving complex problems, developing innovative solutions, and bringing creative ideas to life through code. My dedication to continuous learning has enabled me to build projects that merge technology and creativity.
I am eager to contribute my skills to impactful projects, collaborate with like-minded professionals, and explore new software and game development opportunities. If you're looking for someone passionate about technology and problem-solving, let's connect!
